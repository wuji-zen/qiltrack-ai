Common questions

What is Qiltrack AI? +
Qiltrack AI is an educational platform that uses AI to help you understand companies quickly. We analyze financial data and present it in clear, easy-to-understand reports with multiple perspectives.
Is this investment advice? +
No. Qiltrack AI is an educational tool, not a financial advisor. We help you learn about and explore market data, but we do not provide investment advice or recommendations. Always consult qualified professionals for financial decisions.
What markets are supported? +
We support major exchanges including NYSE, NASDAQ, Hong Kong Stock Exchange, Shanghai, and Shenzhen exchanges. We're continuously expanding our coverage to include more global markets.
How accurate is the AI analysis? +
Our AI uses real-time data from reliable financial sources and applies sophisticated analysis models. However, all analysis is for educational purposes and should be verified independently before making any decisions.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time with no questions asked. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period.
